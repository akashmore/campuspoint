The Ultimate Corner





"Campus Point (The Ultimate Corner)" is an active platform of Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli which is spreading awareness of important notices,upcoming events and important information related to placements in campus. The tag line of our platform is "The Ultimate Corner".We are gathered all the activities which happened across the campus at single corner. We are created different sub platforms in order to give chance to our students and clubs to present their extra curricular activities in front of whole campus.
